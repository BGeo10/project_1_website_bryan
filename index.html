Destiny

Destiny is a first-person shooter that was developed by Bungie, the creators of the famous Halo franchise and was published by Activision. The video game was released worldwide on September 9th,2014 for the PS4, PS3, Xbox One, and Xbox 360 gaming consoles. Unlike Bungie's Halo series that was exclusive to Xbox, Destiny is a franchise for both Playstation and Xbox users to enjoy in a ten-year agreement with Activision. Destiny is set in a science fiction multiplayer "shared-world" environment with some characteristics of role-playing games. The actvities that players can participate in Destiny are divided into player versus environment and player versus player game modes. For short the game modes are (PvE) and (PvP).
